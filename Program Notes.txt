Index

The purpose of the game is to make money trading goods at various ports (4) around the British Isles.  The ship can carry up to 3 different trade goods and a total volume of 25 units


Data Files Details

These are all stored in a folder called Data

Bank – Reference, Bank, Amount
Cargo – Reference, Slot, Trade Good, Amount, Price
Distances (Table) – London, Dover, Edinburgh, Plymouth
Merchant – Reference, Parameter, Value (Data doesn’t change)
Port – Port Name
Trade Goods – Reference, Port, Trade Good, Base Price, Sale Price, Amount, Flag, Purchase Price


Game Flow

Upload Data Files
Post Merchant Description
Sell Trade Goods
Purchase Trade Goods
Reset Prices
Sail to New Port
Option to Continue
